## jog: 

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix} *\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4
\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}
a + 1 & b + 2 \\
c + 3 & d + 4
\end{bmatrix}
$$
biyog just opposite
### example: 
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
8 & 4 & -1 \\
0 & 1 & 3 \\
5 & 4 & 8
\end{bmatrix} +\begin{bmatrix}
-4 & 6 & 2 \\
1 & 3 & 7 \\
5 & 4 & 1
\end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix}
4 & 10 & 1 \\
1 & 4 & 10 \\
10 & 8 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
$$
- binimoy bidhi:
	- a + b = b + a
	- A, B [m x n]
		A + B = B + A 
- shongjojon bidhi:
	- (a + b) + c = a + (b + c)
	- A, B, C = [m x n]
		A + (B + C) = (A + B) + C
